[This Punk Does Not Exist](https://thispunkdoesnotexist.com) is a community tool and art piece for CryptoPunks. It explores the negative space of the collection — the millions of valid trait combinations that were never minted. Each punk is rendered as a pixel-perfect SVG from real CryptoPunk trait layers, composited in the browser and scaled infinitely.

The project is equal parts love letter and thought experiment. Same traits, same grid, same pixel art — but punks that don't exist. A mirror held up to the most iconic NFT collection, showing everything it could have been.
